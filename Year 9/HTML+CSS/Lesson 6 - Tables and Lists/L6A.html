Outline of Genesis 1

  1. Day 1: The Beginning - Genesis 1:1-5
    1. Heavens and the Earth
      1. Earth formless and void
      2. Spirit of God hovering over the waters
    2. Light and Darkness
      1. Light called Day
      2. Darkness called night
  2. Day 2: The Second Day - Genesis 1:6-8
    1. Expanse
      1. Expanse in the midst of the waters
      2. Expanse called Heaven
    2. Waters
      1. Waters above the Expanse
      2. Waters below the Expanse
  3. Day 3: The Third Day - Genesis 1:9-13
    1. Seas and Land
      1. Waters called Seas
      2. Dry Land called Earth
    2. Vegetation
      1. Plants yielding seed by its own kind
      2. Fruit trees by its own kind
  4. Day 4: The Fourth Day - Genesis 1:14-19
    1. Lights to separate day and night in the heavens
      1. Greater light called sunlight
      2. Lesser light called moonlight
    2. Signs for seasons, days and years
      1. Stars
      2. Lights
  5. Day 5: The Fifth Day - Genesis 1:20-23
    1. Water-Breathing Living Creatures
      1. Be fruitful and multiply
      2. Fill the waters in the seas
    2. Birds
      1. Fly above the earth across the expanse of the heavens
      2. Multiply on the earth
  6. Day 7: The Rest - Genesis 2:1-3
    1. God rests
      1. God finished his creation
      2. God would rest from His creative works upon that day
    2. The Purpose of the Sabbath Day
      1. God blesses the Sabbath Day
      2. God sanctified the Sabath Day
  7. Day 6: The Sixth Day - Genesis 1:24-31
    1. Dry Land Creatures
      1. Livestock
      2. Beasts
    2. Mankind
      1. Dominates living things
      2. Plants for food
